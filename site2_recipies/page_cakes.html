Cakes? Biscuits? Sugar...



And now, for all the sweet tooth, we present:



Prep Time Cook Time Serves
10 min 20 min 8 pers

Ingredients

  • Butter or oil, for greasing
  • 50g plain flour
  • 3 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 4 eggs, separated
  • 175g caster sugar
  • For the filling
  • 295g can mandarin segment, drained
  • 200g tub low-fat fromage frais
  • Icing sugar, for dusting

Method

  1. Heat oven to 180C/fan 160C/gas 4.
    2. Grease then line the base and sides of 2 x 20cm sandwich tins with greaseproof paper.
    Sieve the flours and baking powder together.
  2. Use electric hand beaters to whisk the egg whites until stiff, then briefly whisk in the sugar.
  3. Beat the egg yolks quickly, then whisk into the whites.
  4. Fold in the dry ingredients using a large metal spoon, then spoon the mixture into the tins and level the tops.
  5. Bake for 18-20 mins until risen,light golden and a skewer inserted into the middle comes out clean.
  6. Cool in the tins for 10 mins, then gently remove and leave to cool completely.
  7. Mix the mandarins and fromage frais together. Peel away the greaseproof paper, sandwich the cakes with the mandarin mix, then dust with the icing sugar to serve.

Ingredients

Prep Time Cook Time Makes
15 min 20 min 34 biscuits

  • 4 egg whites
  • 140g caster sugar
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 140g ground almond

Method

  1. Heat oven to 160C/140C fan/gas 3.
  2. Line 2 baking trays with baking parchment.
  3. Place the egg whites in a clean bowl and whisk until they form soft peaks.
  4. Add the sugar and continue to whisk for a few mins more until the whites are glossy.
  5. Sift the flour onto the whites, spoon over the almonds, then fold everything together quickly, keeping the mixture light and airy.
  6. Spoon tbsps of the almond mix, well spaced apart, onto the prepared trays.
  7. Bake for 20 mins until golden.
  8. Remove from the oven and cool on a wire rack.
    9. Serve with the gooseberry fool.
    These biscuits are best freshly baked but will keep in a sealed container for up to a week.
